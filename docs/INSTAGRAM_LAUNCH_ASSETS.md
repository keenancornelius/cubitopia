# Cubitopia Instagram Launch Assets Brief

Visual specifications and content strategy for Instagram launch announcement, stories, and highlights. All specs use Instagram's native dimensions.

---

## 1. Launch Announcement Post

### Spec: Feed Post (1080px × 1080px)

**Format**: Static image or short video (under 10 sec)

**Visual Layout**:
- **Background**: Dark gradient (deep blue to black, bottom-left to top-right) representing game atmosphere
- **Game Logo**: Center-top, 300px wide, rendered in metallic gold/silver with subtle glow
- **"NOW LIVE" Badge**: Positioned above logo, bold red/orange text with drop shadow, 120px height
  - Font: Heavy sans-serif (Impact, Bebas, or similar)
  - Color: #FF4500 or bright red with black stroke (3-5px)
  - Optional: Animated version for video post (text zooms in or pulses)

- **Game Preview**: Below logo (400px × 400px)
  - Screenshot of 1v1 match in progress showing:
    - Two opposing camps/bases
    - Units in combat
    - One tribe's units (e.g., Crimson or Viridian) highlighted
  - Border: Thin gold frame (2-3px) to separate from background
  - Slight glow/lighting effect around edges

- **Play Link**: Bottom center, white text on button-like shape
  - URL: https://cubitopia.itch.io or custom domain
  - Font size: Large, easily readable on mobile
  - Background: Semi-transparent dark with border highlight
  - Text: "Play Free Now" or "Join Battle"

- **Tribe Showcase**: Bottom corners (optional, if space)
  - 4-5 tribe icons (40px each) arranged in row below link
  - Represents faction diversity
  - Example: Red, Green, Blue, Purple, Yellow tribe symbols

**Caption**: 300-400 characters
```
Cubitopia is LIVE! Command your tribe in real-time multiplayer combat.
Build. Battle. Dominate.

1v1 | FFA | vs AI
Pick your tribe:
- Crimson Warband
- Viridian Grove
- Azure Order
- And 6 more...

Play free, unlock skins through battle.
[Link in bio]

#Cubitopia #GameLaunch #IndieGames #RTS #WebGame #FreeGames
```

**Hashtags**: Research and refine list, aim for 25-30 relevant tags
- Popular indie game tags
- Genre tags (RTS, strategy, multiplayer)
- Platform tags (browser games, HTML5)
- Community tags (gamedev, indiedev, etc.)

## 2. Story Announcement

### Spec: Story Frame (1080px × 1920px)

**Format**: Static image, repeating sticker animation, or full video (max 15 sec)

**Visual 1: Countdown Teaser** (Pre-launch version, use 1 day before)
- Large animated countdown timer (5-4-3-2-1-LIVE)
- Pulsing game logo center
- Text: "Cubitopia launches in 1 day"
- Background: Action-packed gameplay footage (blurred slightly for video background)

**Visual 2: "NOW LIVE" Announcement** (Launch day & after)
- Bold "NOW LIVE" text (size: 250px+, center-top)
- Game logo (200px, center)
- Animated tribe units racing across screen (optional, loop video)
- Call-to-action: "Tap to Play" sticker pointing to link
- Background gradient same as feed post

**Visual 3: Tribe Feature Highlight** (Day 1-7 rotation)
- Full-screen view of one tribe (rotating daily: Crimson, Viridian, Azure, etc.)
- Tribe name large at top (color-coded to tribe)
- 2-3 key units displayed with brief description
- Special ability called out ("Crimson Warriors: +10% combat damage")
- Swipe-up link: "Pick Your Tribe Now"
- Background: Tribe thematic colors and environment

**Visual 4: Gameplay Loop** (Video, 8-12 sec)
- Quick montage: queue → match start → combat → victory screen → unlock reward
- Fast cuts with upbeat energy
- Overlay text: "Fast matches. Real competition. No p2w."
- End frame: "Play Free" with link sticker

**Story-Only Callouts**:
- Mention Stripe purchase option ("Unlock tribe skins")
- Highlight multiplayer matchmaking speed
- Showcase tribe diversity and customization
- Encourage Discord/Reddit joins
- Poll sticker: "What's your tribe?" or "First match: Win or Loss?"
- Question sticker: "What's your favorite strategy?"

## 3. Profile Picture (Profile Icon)

### Spec: Square Profile Icon (300px × 300px minimum, circular-safe)

**Design**:
- Game logo or icon as primary element
- Ensure design is readable at 32px (smallest profile display size)
- Circle-safe area: Keep all important elements within center 260px (to avoid crop on circular profile frames)

**Options**:
- **Option A**: Game logo alone, centered
  - Solid background (dark color or brand color)
  - High contrast for visibility

- **Option B**: Simplified tribal icon (choose dominant tribe or create neutral symbol)
  - Single clear shape, minimal detail
  - Bold colors

- **Option C**: Stylized "C" for Cubitopia
  - Modern, geometric design
  - Color: Gradient or solid brand color

**Requirements**:
- No transparency (use opaque background)
- 300px × 300px recommended (Instagram auto-crops)
- Readable on mobile at 32px-64px size
- Consistent with website/Discord branding

## 5. Highlight Covers (Story Highlight Icons)

### Spec: Circle Highlight Cover (1080px × 1350px final, but design for 1080px × 1080px safe area)

Story Highlights are persistent pinned collections on your profile. Create highlight covers for:

#### Highlight 1: "Tribes"
- **Cover**: 4-grid layout showing 4 different tribe units (Crimson, Viridian, Azure, and 1 other)
- **Background**: Dark with gold dividing lines
- **Text**: "TRIBES" (bottom, centered, 80px font)
- **Purpose**: Organized stories rotating through tribe features/guides

#### Highlight 2: "Gameplay"
- **Cover**: Action screenshot of live 1v1 match (two bases, units fighting)
- **Foreground text**: "GAMEPLAY" (bottom-right, 80px, white with black stroke)
- **Purpose**: Gameplay highlights, match moments, combat mechanics

#### Highlight 3: "Updates"
- **Cover**: Roadmap/development theme (checkmarks, roadmap visual)
- **Text**: "UPDATES" (bottom-left, 80px, green/accent color)
- **Background**: Clean, modern layout suggesting progress
- **Purpose**: Patch notes, new features, balance changes

#### Highlight 4: "Community"
- **Cover**: 4-player grid or team celebration visual
- **Text**: "COMMUNITY" (bottom, centered, 80px)
- **Background**: Social, inclusive theme
- **Purpose**: Clips of player wins, community moments, Discord announcements

#### Highlight 5: "Tournaments" (Optional, future)
- **Cover**: Podium/trophy visual, leaderboard style
- **Text**: "TOURNAMENTS" (bottom, 80px)
- **Purpose**: Competitive events, ELO rankings, player spotlights

**Highlight Cover Design Requirements**:
- All text readable at 64px size (smallest display on profile)
- Consistent font and style across all covers
- Color-coded for quick recognition (Tribes = red border, Updates = green, etc.)
- High contrast background so text pops

## 6. Reels Strategy (Bonus)

Instagram Reels are high-engagement format. Plan 2-3 launch week reels:

**Reel 1: "60-Second Match" (10-15 sec)**
- Full gameplay match compressed to show: build → expand → attack → victory
- Text overlay: "Real matches. Real strategy. 60 seconds."
- End frame: "Play free: [link]"
- Audio: Upbeat, trending sound (or in-game audio if copyright-safe)

**Reel 2: "Tribe Introductions" (15-20 sec)**
- Flash through 2-3 tribes (2-3 sec each)
- Showcase unique units of each tribe
- Text: "9 tribes. 9 play styles. Pick yours."
- End: CTA with link

**Reel 3: "Player Reaction" (User-generated or your playtest)**
- 8-10 sec of live gameplay with narrator (you or team member) explaining cool moments
- "Watch this combo!" / "Multiunit garrison incoming!"
- Casual, energetic tone
- End: "Your turn. Play now."

**Reel Tips**:
- Use trending audio (check Instagram Sounds library)
- Post Reels on Friday evening (higher weekend engagement)
- Reels boost post reach 40-60% higher than static posts
- Include #Reels hashtag and game hashtags

## Design Asset Checklist

Before creating visuals, confirm:

- [ ] Game logo exported (300px, 1080px, 1500px variants; PNG with transparency)
- [ ] Tribe icons/symbols finalized (at least 32px readable)
- [ ] In-game screenshots/gameplay footage captured (minimum 1080p resolution)
- [ ] Color palette locked (primary colors, accent colors, text colors)
- [ ] Font choices finalized (Instagram supports system fonts; avoid rare custom fonts for stories)
- [ ] Tribe unit artwork isolated for highlights (PSD layers or separate exports)
- [ ] Video editing software ready (Premiere, CapCut, Adobe Express, or similar)
- [ ] Linktree/link-in-bio platform set up and tested
- [ ] Hashtag list researched and tested (preview each on Instagram)
- [ ] Caption copy written and proofread
- [ ] Posting schedule determined (time zone, frequency)

## Production Timeline

- **3 days before launch**: Launch announcement post + 2 story frames ready
- **1 day before**: All highlight covers created; bio updated; link tested
- **Launch day**: Post announcement; activate stories; refresh every 4 hours
- **Days 1-7**: Daily stories + 3-4 feed posts + 2 reels
- **Week 2**: Stabilize to 1-2 posts/day + 1 reel/week

---

## Success Metrics (Track Weekly)

- **Reach**: Total accounts reached (target: 500+ day 1)
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares (target: > 5% engagement rate)
- **Link clicks**: Traffic to game URL (track via Google Analytics)
- **Follower growth**: Daily follower increase (target: +50-100/day launch week)
- **Story completion**: % of viewers who reach end of story (target: > 60%)
- **Reel plays**: Total plays on video content (target: 1000+ per reel)

Monitor daily and adjust content based on performance. Prioritize formats with highest engagement for future posts.
