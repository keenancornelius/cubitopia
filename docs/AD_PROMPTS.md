## Usage Guidelines

### Platform Recommendations by Prompt Type

**1. Unit Trash Talk**
- Best for: Reels, Shorts, TikTok, Quick Clips
- Frequency: 3-4x per week
- Caption ideas: "POV: You trash talk in Cubitopia", "We all have that one unit...", "Confidence level: 100%"

**2. Dramatic Movie Trailer Parody**
- Best for: YouTube, Launch announcements, Major events
- Frequency: 1-2x per month (or campaign-dependent)
- Caption ideas: "Coming Soon", "In a world...", "The battle begins"

**3. Expectation vs Reality**
- Best for: Reddit, Twitter, Instagram Memes
- Frequency: 2-3x per week
- Caption ideas: "When you plan vs when you execute", "#relatablecontent", "Is this just me?"

**4. Satirical Mobile Game Ads**
- Best for: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- Frequency: 1-2x per week
- Caption ideas: "POV: Other mobile games", "We don't do this", "Honesty in gaming"

**5. Tribe Reveal Cards**
- Best for: Website, Discord, Dedicated card reveal series
- Frequency: Weekly (one tribe per week for 9 weeks)
- Caption ideas: "Meet the [TRIBE]", "Which tribe are you?", Tribe origin stories

**6. Social Media Banners**
- Best for: Static profile decoration, permanent placement
- Frequency: One-time per platform (update seasonally)
- Caption ideas: Use in profile bio/description rather than captions

### Content Mixing Strategy

- **Daily content cycle:** Rotate between Unit Trash Talk and short meme clips
- **Weekly features:** Tribe card reveals, strategy tips
- **Monthly events:** Trailer parody drops, major announcements
- **Seasonal:** Banner updates, new aesthetic themes

### Monetization Tie-In

Each of these content types can highlight cosmetic purchases:
- **Tribe cards:** Feature exclusive cosmetic skins
- **Banners:** Subtle cosmetic cosmetic placement
- **Trash talk clips:** Units wearing rare skins
- **Movie trailers:** Showcase visual spectacle (cosmetics enhance this)

## Notes for Content Creators

- All prompts are designed to work with Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion
- Consider commissioning a skilled voxel artist to create consistent branded versions
- Use consistent color grading across all content for brand cohesion
- Test prompts with multiple AI tools—results vary by platform
- Always include "Cubitopia" branding subtly to improve discoverability
- Watermark generated content appropriately
- Credit AI tools in video descriptions when required
