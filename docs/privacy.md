# Privacy Policy for Cubitopia

**Effective Date:** [Placeholder — Update before launch]

## 1. Introduction

Cubitopia ("we," "us," "our," or "the Game") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you play Cubitopia.

Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the Game.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Anonymous Authentication
Cubitopia uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identifying information. You are identified within the Game only by an anonymous session ID generated by Firebase.

### 2.2 Game Data
We collect and store the following anonymous game statistics:
- ELO rating and competitive rank
- Match history (wins, losses, game outcomes)
- Tribe selection and cosmetic choices
- In-game progression and building statistics
- Game session timestamps and duration

All game data is associated only with your anonymous Firebase session ID, not with you as a person.

### 2.3 Payment Information
When you purchase tribe skin cosmetics through Stripe, **we do not handle or store your payment card data**. Stripe processes all payments directly and securely. We receive only:
- Confirmation of successful purchase
- Tribe skin item purchased
- Purchase timestamp
- Transaction ID (for support purposes)

Your credit card, bank account, and other payment details are processed entirely by Stripe and are subject to Stripe's privacy policy.

### 2.4 Technical Information
To operate the Game, we may automatically collect:
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- IP address (for login and abuse prevention)
- Game client version
- Crash reports and error logs

## 3. No Advertising or Tracking Cookies

Cubitopia does not:
- Display advertisements or third-party ads
- Use tracking cookies or external analytics services
- Integrate with social media trackers or ad networks
- Sell your data or game statistics to advertisers

We use only essential session management cookies required for the Game to function properly.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain Cubitopia's online multiplayer service
- Calculate and display your ELO rating and match statistics
- Process your in-game purchases via Stripe
- Prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse
- Improve Game performance and stability
- Debug technical issues and errors
- Comply with applicable laws

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except:

### 5.1 Stripe
Payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe, Inc. Stripe's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.

### 5.2 Firebase
Game authentication and session management use Google Firebase. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

## 6. COPPA Compliance (Children's Online Privacy Protection)

Cubitopia is designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we use anonymous authentication and do not collect personal data (names, emails, etc.), COPPA compliance is inherent to our design.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information to Cubitopia, please contact us immediately at support@cubitopia.com.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Game Statistics**: Anonymous ELO ratings and match history are retained for the lifetime of your account.
- **Firebase Session IDs**: Retained as long as your account is active.
- **Payment Data**: Processed and stored by Stripe according to their retention policies (typically 7 years for payment records).

**Account Deletion**: If you wish to delete your account and associated game statistics, contact support@cubitopia.com. We will delete your anonymous game data while retaining transaction records as required by law.

## 8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:
- Anonymous session IDs are encrypted in transit and at rest
- Communication with our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- Payment data is never stored on our servers (processed by Stripe)
- Regular security reviews and updates

However, no security system is completely impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access your game statistics (available in-game)
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Object to certain uses of your data
- Data portability (export of your game statistics)

To exercise these rights, contact support@cubitopia.com.

## 10. International Users

If you access Cubitopia from outside your home country, you consent to the transfer of your information to our servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. Different countries have different privacy laws, and your data may receive different levels of protection depending on location.
